Best Movies According to Patrick

My top 3 movies of all times.

Gone In 60 Seconds

This is an outstanding story of a brother who had heathertofore been involved in grand theft auto but had since retired. He finds himself dragged back in the trade, this time to save his young brother who by now is involved in the act.

In the Darkest Hour

A story about England's toughest prime minister, Sir Churchhill in time of war and how he had to make tough decisions that would later prove particularly popular amongst the general public.

Passengers

In a quest to venture in a futuristic voyage and life on a different planet, two Passengers find themselves trapped due to the malfunctioning of the ship. They are forced to stay awake for years so as to see the rest of the Passengers on the ship safely to their destination.
